# Glossary of Zero-Click Law Terminology
**Supplementary Document** **Author:** DrewIs Intelligence LLC  
**Date:** January 2026

[cite_start]This glossary provides standardized definitions for terms used in the **Zero-Click Law System**, including the *Zero-Click Laws v1.0* and the *Structural Authority Standard v1.0*[cite: 610, 665].

---

## 1. Core Paradigm Concepts

* [cite_start]**Zero-Click Paradigm:** The fundamental restructuring of digital discovery where a significant majority of search engine queries (currently exceeding 58%) are resolved directly on the search results page via AI Overviews, Featured Snippets, or Knowledge Panels, resulting in no organic click to the original source[cite: 613]. [cite_start]This shift prioritizes citation over click-through[cite: 614].
* [cite_start]**Generative Engine Optimization (GEO):** The methodology for optimizing enterprise content to be accurately and authoritatively synthesized and cited by Large Language Models (LLMs) and Generative AI systems[cite: 615]. [cite_start]It focuses on fact density, structured content, and expert quotations[cite: 616].
* [cite_start]**Answer Engine Optimization (AEO):** The technical application layer focused on implementing specific structured data protocols (Schema Markup) and content architecture to make content machine-readable and preferential for AI extraction[cite: 618].
* [cite_start]**Default Recommendation Status:** The strategic goal of achieving consistent citation by AI Overviews and LLMs as the authoritative, default source for specific topics or transactions[cite: 620].
* [cite_start]**AI Mode:** A distinct, end-to-end AI search experience (launched in Google Search, May 2025) that replaces traditional "10 blue links" with a conversational, citation-based "Consideration Set"[cite: 624].
* [cite_start]**Citation Frequency:** The primary success metric under the Zero-Click Paradigm, replacing click-through rate (CTR) as the key indicator of algorithmic authority[cite: 622, 623].

## 2. Quantitative Metrics

* [cite_start]**CCR (CTR Compression Ratio):** The core metric for measuring click-through rate compression, calculated as $CTR_{current} / CTR_{baseline}$[cite: 629]. [cite_start]Trigger threshold: $\le 0.80$ sustained for $\ge 14$ days[cite: 631].
* [cite_start]**DI (Displacement Index):** A metric quantifying the gap between visibility and traffic, calculated as $(\Delta Sessions\% - \Delta Impressions\%)$[cite: 632]. [cite_start]A negative DI is the defining characteristic of traffic displacement[cite: 633].
* [cite_start]**TIE (Total Interaction Equity):** A metric measuring traffic-per-impression efficiency, calculated as $Sessions / Impressions$[cite: 635].
* [cite_start]**Compounding Flag:** An indicator tracking consecutive measurement windows with a negative Displacement Index to identify accelerating displacement[cite: 643].

## 3. Technical Standards

* [cite_start]**The Gold Structure:** The foundational multi-site architecture separating research authority from commercial activities[cite: 647]. [cite_start]It consists of five core principles: Entity Clarity, Research Purity, Commercial Clarity, Product Independence, and Strategic Interconnection[cite: 648].
* [cite_start]**Research Purity:** A Gold Structure principle requiring complete separation of research and thought leadership from commercial sales, ensuring properties are independently citable[cite: 657, 658].
* [cite_start]**Schema Interconnection:** The requirement that all properties be linked through schema markup (specifically `sameAs` and `parentOrganization`) to create a traversable knowledge graph for AI systems[cite: 659, 660].
* [cite_start]**Answer-First Format:** A content requirement that question-based pages provide a direct, concise answer (40â€“60 words) immediately following the question heading to optimize for snippet extraction[cite: 661, 662].
* [cite_start]**E-E-A-T Signals:** Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness indicators required for AEO compliance, including author credentials and authoritative backlinks[cite: 663, 664].
